OUR STORY

Two Founders

Two Tugs Brewing was brought to life through the shared passion and vision of its two founders, Ben Hayes and Mark Thompson. With a focus on sustainability and a commitment to sourcing local ingredients, Mark's business acumen complements Ben's craft, making Two Tugs Brewing not only a destination for exceptional beer but also a cornerstone of the local community's social tapestry.
